# Legal Boundaries

This project must stay within a private social platform and concierge support model.

## Non-negotiables

1. No marriage promises.
2. No relationship guarantees.
3. No assigned partners.
4. No member-as-inventory language.
5. No outcome-based romantic fee language.
6. No implied intimate access.
7. No pressure on members to communicate or meet.
8. No public display of private member information without consent.
9. No marketing without proper consent and opt-out handling.
10. No retention of identity documents beyond approved policy.

## Required disclaimer

Real Match does not arrange marriages, assign partners, guarantee relationships, or broker romantic outcomes. All connections are independently initiated and mutually accepted by verified adult members.

## Review rule

Any change to pricing, member discovery, verification, messaging, or meeting coordination requires a legal and safety review before release.
