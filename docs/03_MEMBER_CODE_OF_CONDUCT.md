# Member Code of Conduct

Real Match requires respectful, truthful, and consent-based behavior from every member.

## Required conduct

- Provide accurate identity and profile information.
- Communicate respectfully.
- Respect every decline immediately.
- Do not pressure anyone to communicate, meet, continue contact, or share private information.
- Do not use financial pressure, gifts, favors, employment, travel, or status to influence another member.
- Do not ask staff to force, steer, rank, or assign another member.
- Do not create fake profiles or represent someone else.

## Prohibited behavior

- Harassment or intimidation.
- Coercion or manipulation.
- Any expectation of intimate access.
- Transactional romantic demands.
- Threats, stalking, doxxing, or retaliation.
- Circumventing platform safeguards.

## Enforcement

The platform may reject, suspend, remove, or escalate any member whose conduct creates safety, legal, trust, or reputational risk.
