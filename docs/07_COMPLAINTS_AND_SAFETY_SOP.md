# Complaints and Safety SOP

Complaints and safety events must be documented, triaged, and resolved with clear ownership.

## Intake

Every report should capture:

- reporter ID
- reported member ID, if applicable
- issue type
- severity
- date and time
- evidence summary
- immediate safety action
- assigned owner

## Severity levels

- Low: minor conduct issue or unclear concern.
- Medium: repeated boundary issue, pressure, or trust concern.
- High: safety threat, harassment, coercion, fraud concern, or legal escalation.

## Required actions

- Acknowledge reports quickly.
- Pause member visibility if risk is unclear.
- Preserve only necessary evidence.
- Escalate high-risk cases to leadership and counsel.
- Record final action and resolution.

## Non-negotiable

No revenue target overrides member safety.
