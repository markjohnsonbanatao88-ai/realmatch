# Product Positioning

Real Match is a private verified social-introduction and cultural concierge platform for consenting adults.

## Safe positioning

Use language that emphasizes:

- identity verification readiness
- profile consultation
- respectful social discovery
- member standards
- independent mutual interest
- optional logistical concierge coordination

## Required public boundary

Real Match does not arrange marriages, assign partners, guarantee relationships, or broker romantic outcomes. All connections are independently initiated and mutually accepted by verified adult members.

## Forbidden positioning

Do not frame the service as:

- a marriage agency
- a guaranteed relationship service
- a platform that sells access to people
- a service that assigns partners
- a service that promises meetings, chemistry, marriage, sex, companionship, or outcomes

## Operating truth

The product must behave exactly like the public positioning. If staff manually pressure, assign, or steer members while the site says user-driven, the business is misaligned.
