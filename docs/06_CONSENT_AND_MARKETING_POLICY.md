# Consent and Marketing Policy

Real Match must separate service contact from marketing contact.

## Consent types

- Service contact consent: required to respond to a private assessment.
- Marketing contact consent: optional and must be recorded separately.
- Terms acceptance: required before any live account or paid workflow.
- Conduct agreement: required before any member visibility or connection flow.

## Records to keep

Each consent record should include:

- person ID
- consent type
- consent value
- source
- timestamp
- terms or policy version
- withdrawal status, if applicable

## Marketing rules

- Do not send marketing messages to people without a clear lawful basis and recorded consent where required.
- Every marketing message must identify the sender.
- Every marketing message must include a simple opt-out path.
- Opt-outs must be honored promptly and logged.

## Release gate

No mass outreach should run until consent source, suppression list, message templates, and review ownership are documented.
