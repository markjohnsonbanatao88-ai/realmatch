# Coordination SOP

Coordination is optional logistical support only.

## Allowed support

- Confirm both members independently requested support.
- Coordinate public venue options.
- Support scheduling.
- Provide cultural and communication guidance.
- Share safety check-in expectations.
- Record consent and completion status.

## Prohibited support

- Do not pressure a member to meet.
- Do not promise chemistry, romance, continuation, or outcome.
- Do not coordinate private or unsafe venues as the default.
- Do not hide safety concerns from either member.
- Do not accept side payments for special treatment.

## Required record

Each coordination event should record:

- requesting member ID
- other member ID
- mutual confirmation
- date and venue
- assigned coordinator
- safety notes
- completion status
- incident report reference, if any

## Release gate

Coordination must not go live until member consent records, staff playbooks, complaint handling, and privacy controls are ready.
