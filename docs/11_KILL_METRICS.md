# Kill Metrics

These conditions require pause, review, or project shutdown until corrected.

## Immediate pause triggers

- Members or leads repeatedly misunderstand the product as an outcome-guarantee service.
- Staff use prohibited language in sales or operations.
- Safety complaints cannot be handled within the documented workflow.
- Privacy or retention controls are not ready for real data.
- Payment descriptions drift away from consultation or coordination services.
- Marketing lacks consent source, opt-out path, or suppression records.
- Any member is pressured to communicate, meet, continue, or disclose private details.

## Review triggers

- High complaint rate.
- Unclear refund expectations.
- Confusion about what the fees cover.
- Staff manually steer people in ways not documented.
- Users expect outcomes the platform does not sell.

## Principle

If revenue requires weakening consent, safety, privacy, or legal boundaries, the model is not ready to scale.
