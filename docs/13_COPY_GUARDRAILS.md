# Copy Guardrails

## Approved language

- Private verified social introductions
- Cultural concierge support
- Private assessment
- Review readiness
- Profile consultation
- Member standards
- Independent mutual interest
- Optional logistical coordination
- Consent-based connection flow

## Prohibited language

- Guaranteed relationship
- Guaranteed meeting
- Guaranteed chemistry
- Assigned partner
- Buy access to members
- Special access to people
- Outcome fee
- Romantic success fee
- Intimate access
- Inventory of members

## Pricing language

Use:

- Private Assessment — Free
- Verification & Profile Consultation — £299
- Optional Concierge Coordination — £600

Do not describe the £600 fee as an outcome-based fee. It covers logistics only.

## Review checklist

Before publishing copy, confirm:

- The user-driven nature is clear.
- The disclaimer is visible on conversion pages.
- Members are described with dignity.
- No outcome is promised.
- No group is treated as a product.
